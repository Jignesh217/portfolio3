About Me

Hello! I'm a passionate web developer with a strong interest in crafting intuitive and visually engaging digital experiences. With a background in computer science and years of hands-on experience, I've honed my skills in frontend technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, while also dabbling in backend development using Node.js and databases like MongoDB. I love turning ideas into real products and continuously push myself to stay updated with the latest industry trends and tools. My goal is to build applications that not only function well but also bring joy to users. When I'm not coding, I enjoy contributing to open-source projects, reading tech blogs, and mentoring aspiring developers.
